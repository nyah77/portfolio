Animate project 3

Project Summary

The purpose of this project was to create a digital advertisement for a skincare brand using Adobe Animate. The goal was to design an engaging and visually appealing animation that effectively communicates the brand’s values while promoting its products. This project focused on combining graphic design elements, motion, and timing to capture the viewer’s attention and deliver a clear message. By using animation, the ad aimed to highlight the eco-friendly and modern qualities of the brand.

Explanation of tools and skills

For this project, I started by importing the background, the woman’s face, and the wind vector into separate layers on the stage so I could animate each one individually. I created motion tweens for the wind, which made it look like a gust blowing the woman away. To add more detail, I imported a dust image and set the blend mode to darken so parts of the dust would be transparent and look more natural in the scene. I also added a glow around the product to make it stand out and gave the product a motion tween so it slid into the stage when it appeared. To bring more impact to that moment, I added a sound effect that plays as the product and glow show up, and I layered in background music to tie the whole animation together. By combining layers, motion tweens, blend modes, effects, and audio, I was able to create a more dynamic and engaging ad.
