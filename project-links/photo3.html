Photoshop project 3

Project Summary

The purpose of this project was to practice advanced photo restoration techniques in Photoshop, transforming a damaged or faded image into a clearer and more visually appealing version. This exercise focused on preserving the original photograph’s historical and sentimental value while improving its overall quality. By restoring the image, the project demonstrated how digital tools can be used to extend the life of old photographs and make them more meaningful for modern viewing.

Explanation of tools and skills

For this project, I used the Spot Healing Brush Tool and Healing Brush Tool to remove scratches, water spots, and blemishes while preserving natural textures. I converted the image to a Smart Object to enable non-destructive editing and applied the Neural Filter – Colorize for initial colorization. To refine the AI-generated color, I manually painted in missing or inaccurate areas using sampled colors and created precise selections with the Magnetic Lasso Tool to adjust the bricks around the fireplace. I then used Hue/Saturation adjustment layers to fine-tune color, saturation, and brightness. Throughout the process, I revisited healing and retouching tools to ensure a clean, polished result, demonstrating careful attention to detail and mastery of both AI-assisted and manual editing techniques.
